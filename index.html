Business Support Consulting

6. Integrated Pricing Flyer

Standard Notarization Fees (NJ Statutory)

  • $2.50 per signature notarized.
  • Applies to acknowledgments, jurats, oaths/affirmations, affidavits, identity statements, consent forms.

Mobile Notary Travel Fees

Distance from Willingboro, NJ Fee Range
Local (0–10 miles) $15–$25
Mid‑range (11–20 miles) $25–$40
Extended (21–35 miles) $40–$60
Emergency / Same‑Day + $15 surcharge

Travel fees are separate from notarization fees and are due even if the notarization cannot be completed due to signer issues (ID, willingness, document readiness).

Remote Online Notary (RON) Pricing

  • $25–$35 per notarization
  • + $10 per additional seal
  • + $10 per additional signer
  • Includes identity verification, digital certificate, secure recording, and audit trail.

Loan Signing Services

Package Type Fee Range
Seller Package $75–$100
Buyer Package $100–$150
Refinance Package $100–$150
HELOC $85–$125
Loan Modifications $75–$100

Additional Services

  • Document printing: $0.25 per page (B&W) / $0.50 per page (Color).
  • Scan‑backs: $10–$25 depending on volume.
  • Witness services: $10 per witness (if provided).
  • Form completion assistance: $15–$50 depending on complexity.

Service Area

  • Willingboro
  • Mount Holly
