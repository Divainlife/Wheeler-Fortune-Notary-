Intake Questionnaire

Part 1: Baseline Technical Proficiency

  • Rate your current proficiency with the following tools (1 = Beginner, 5 = Can build from scratch without tutorials): SQL, Advanced Excel, BI Tools, Modeling Software, Scripting.
  • In 3–4 sentences, describe the most complex technical project, data model, or dashboard you have built entirely on your own.

Part 2: Scenario-Based Autonomy & Ambiguity

  • Scenario 1 – The Incomplete Dataset: You are assigned to update a critical, weekly performance model. The report is due tomorrow at 9:00 AM. 15% of the data is missing. Your manager is out of the office. Walk through the exact steps you take.
  • Scenario 2 – The Vague Stakeholder: A VP sends an email: “I need a dashboard that shows how our department is doing this quarter compared to last year. Get it to me by Friday.” Draft your exact email reply to scope the requirements.
  • Scenario 3 – Conflicting Priorities: You are working on an engineering integration due Wednesday. The Director of Finance asks for an urgent data pull for a presentation happening at 2:00 PM today. How do you handle the competing deadlines?

Part 3: Quality Control & Governance

  • Scenario 4 – The Production Error: You built a model that tracks project costs. It was sent to leadership yesterday. Today, you realize you made a calculation error skewing the estimate by 5%. No one else has noticed. What are your immediate next three steps?
  • Scenario 5 – The Validation Process: Before you send a finalized dataset to a senior stakeholder, what is your personal, step-by-step process for validating that your output is 100% accurate?

Part 4: Self-Awareness & Growth

  • Looking at target roles, what is the single technical requirement or software that intimidates you the most, and how are you currently trying to learn it?
  • On a scale of 1 to 10, how comfortable are you taking ownership of a project where the rules and deliverables are not clearly defined by your manager?
